How to type Urdu using English letters

  • Enter a Roman Urdu word in the editor above—for example, type mera.
  • Press Space to convert the word automatically into Urdu script.
  • If the result is not the word you intended, press Backspace twice to open alternative suggestions.
  • Use Copy text when you are ready to paste your Urdu into another app.
  • To type "میرا خیال ہے" in Urdu script, enter mera khayal hai and press Space after each word.
  • You can type names and short messages in the same way before sharing them on social media.
  • To add headings, colours, lists or other formatting, open the Urdu Rich Text Editor.
  • No Urdu keyboard software is required; the editor runs directly in your browser.
  • Visit the Urdu FAQ for concise answers about the language and writing system.
