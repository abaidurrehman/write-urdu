Write and format Urdu with the rich-text editor

  • Type a Roman Urdu word and press Space to convert it into Urdu script.
  • Paste a longer Roman Urdu passage, then choose Convert all text to transliterate it in one step.
  • If the result is not what you expected, press Backspace twice and choose a suggestion.
  • Select text to change its font, size, colour, alignment or emphasis.
  • Use the editor’s Edit menu—or your usual keyboard shortcut—to copy formatted text.
  • Use Export to download a Word document, PDF or PNG image, or to print your document.

What you can create

  • Polished Urdu documents, letters and reports for Word.
  • Right-to-left HTML content for websites and web applications.
  • Headings, quotations and body text for Urdu articles or blog posts.
  • Clear, styled Urdu messages for email, presentations and social media.
