Type and format Urdu in the rich-text editor

  • Type a Roman Urdu word, then press Space to convert it into Urdu script.
  • If the result is incorrect, press Backspace twice and choose from the suggested words.
  • Select text to change its font, size, colour, alignment or emphasis.
  • Use the editor's Edit menu—or your usual keyboard shortcut—to copy the formatted text.
  • Use Export to download a Word document, PDF or PNG image, or to print your work.

What you can create

  • Formatted Urdu text for Word documents, letters and reports.
  • Right-to-left HTML content for websites and web applications.
  • Headings, quotations and body copy for Urdu articles or blog posts.
  • Styled Urdu messages for email, presentations and social media.
