  • Your file will instantly download with the Urdu manuscript

It is a very useful feature. You can copy anything to the clipboard of your device, laptop, MacBook or cell phone. Later you can utilize this copied Urdu text or image to your benefit and paste it anywhere you want to. You can paste it in a document or over the internet or in any file you want to.
