Create Urdu cards and quote images online

The Write Urdu Card Studio is a free browser-based Urdu card maker for quote images, poetry cards, announcements and social posts. Choose an Urdu font and template, add a local background, adjust the design and download the finished card as a PNG.

What you can create

Make a poetry quote, WhatsApp status, Instagram quote, Eid greeting, Ramadan reminder or classroom announcement without a design account.

How it works

Paste Urdu text, or send text from the basic editor, choose a size and template, adjust typography, then export the exact card you see in the preview.

Private local images

Background images are processed in this browser. They are not uploaded; remove them whenever you want and keep the finished PNG on your device.

Output sizes for common posts

Choose Square Post (1080 × 1080), Landscape Post (1280 × 720), Wide Social Post (1200 × 630), or Story / Status (1080 × 1920). Your text and background are reflowed for the selected ratio.

Urdu fonts and direct editing

Templates use Urdu-friendly Noto Nastaliq Urdu and Noto Naskh Arabic styles. Select text on the preview to move or resize it, then edit it with the native text field on desktop or mobile.

PNG export and sharing

The final PNG is rendered at the selected output dimensions. Download it for WhatsApp Status, Instagram, classroom materials, announcements, or personal Urdu poetry cards.

Card Studio questions

Is the Urdu Card Studio free?

Yes. It is available without an account, and the design and PNG rendering run in the browser.

Can I use it on a phone?

Yes. The preview and controls adapt to mobile screens, and direct text editing uses the device's native Urdu keyboard.

Can I add a background image?

Yes. Choose a local JPG, PNG or WebP image. It is processed in your browser and is not uploaded to Write Urdu.

Why does a long quote appear smaller?

Auto-fit reduces the font size when needed to keep the complete quote readable inside the selected card layout. Shorten the text or widen the text box when you want larger type.

Can I create a card from Roman Urdu?

Type Roman Urdu in the basic editor first, then choose Create Urdu Card to send the resulting Urdu text to the Studio without changing your original draft.
