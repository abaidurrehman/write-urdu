How the Urdu Template Library works

These templates are starting points for the browser-based Urdu Card Studio. Your selected design opens locally, without an account or a server request containing your text.

Choose a size for the destination

Square designs suit general posts, landscape designs work well for wide announcements, and story designs are made for WhatsApp Status and vertical social posts. You can change the output preset after opening a template.

Make the design your own

  • Edit the Urdu text and optional author line.
  • Change fonts, colours, alignment, background and watermark settings.
  • Select the text on the preview to move, resize or edit it directly.
  • Download a high-resolution PNG when the card is ready.
Are these templates free to use?

Yes. The starter templates are original Write Urdu designs and are available in the browser without registration. Your finished card remains yours to edit and export.

Does choosing a template upload my text?

No. The template selection is a local route change. Card text, uploaded images and the generated card stay in your browser unless you explicitly export or share them.
